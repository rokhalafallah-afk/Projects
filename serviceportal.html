Student Clubs at MUST

GDG

GDG is the Google Developer Student Club at MUST. Members learn Google technologies, build web and mobile applications, and collaborate on real-world projects. The club hosts workshops, hackathons, and mentoring sessions to develop technical skills and network with tech peers.

01012345678

IEEE

IEEE is the global engineering and technology club at MUST. It supports members through seminars, robotics projects, and research events. The club encourages innovation in electronics, communications, and AI, building professional networks and technical skills.

01012345678

CPC

CPC is the Competitive Programming Club at MUST. It brings together students who love coding, problem-solving, and software development. The club organizes programming contests, collaborative projects, and training sessions to improve coding fluency.

01012345678

Cyber MUST

Cyber MUST is the student cybersecurity club at MUST. It focuses on ethical hacking, digital defense, and cybersecurity research. Members train in CTF challenges, threat analysis, and security labs to build strong protection skills for cyber careers.

01012345678

Music Club

The Music Club is a place for students who love music and creativity. Members can sing, play instruments, and learn about different types of music. We practice together, share talents, and prepare performances to improve confidence and artistic skills.

01012345678

Art Club

The Art Club is a creative space for students who love drawing, painting, and expressing ideas through art. Members explore different styles using colors, pencils, and digital tools. We work on projects to develop creativity, imagination, and confidence.

01012345678

Huawei AI Club

The MUST AI Club is a certified training center qualifying students in Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning using Huawei tools like ModelArts. Activities include HCIA-AI prep camps, practical projects, and the Huawei ICT Competition.

01012345678

Huawei Academy

Huawei Academy at MUST provides certified training connecting university studies with the job market in Cloud, AI, Security, and Datacom. Activities include certified courses, summer internships, job opportunities, and tech seminars with global experts.
